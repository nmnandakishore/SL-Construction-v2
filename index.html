About Us

Engineer-Led Construction Since 2016

SL Construction Company was established in 2016 with the objective of delivering quality construction services supported by strong engineering expertise. The company is managed by a team of three qualified Civil Engineers, all holding Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) degrees, who jointly founded the firm.

Over the past 9+ years, the company has successfully delivered projects across residential and commercial sectors, maintaining high standards of quality, durability, and timely completion.

What We Do

Our Services

Residential Building Construction

From foundation to finishing, we deliver structurally sound homes tailored to your family's needs. Our engineer-led team oversees every phase including layout, masonry, roofing, electrical, plumbing, and interior finishes, ensuring quality and timely handover.

Commercial Building Construction

We build commercial structures such as showrooms, office complexes, and institutional buildings with reinforced frameworks, efficient floor plans, and high-load capacities. Every project meets local building codes and is designed for long-term durability.

Villa Projects

Luxurious and contemporary villas designed around your vision. We handle everything from architectural planning to landscape integration and use premium materials with modern construction techniques to create your ideal home.

RCC Structural Works

Specialized in reinforced cement concrete works including columns, beams, slabs, and foundations. Our designs are engineered for maximum load-bearing capacity and seismic resilience using tested materials and precise formwork.

Waterproofing Works

Protect your investment with our comprehensive waterproofing for terrace, basement, bathroom, and overhead tank treatments. We use industry-grade membranes, sealants, and coatings to prevent leakage and structural degradation.

Renovation & Remodeling

Breathe new life into your property with our renovation services. Whether it's a kitchen remodel, bathroom upgrade, structural modification, or complete interior overhaul, we deliver clean execution with minimal disruption.
